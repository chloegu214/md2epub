送 进 KindleONTO YOUR KINDLE

装订好的 EPUB 是标准格式:通过 Send to Kindle(网页、邮件或 App)发送,亚马逊会自动完成转换并推送到你的设备;新款 Kindle Scribe 用户还可以从 Google Drive 直接导入。

The finished EPUB is a standard file: send it via Send to Kindle (web, email, or app) and Amazon converts and delivers it to your device automatically; newer Kindle Scribe owners can also import straight from Google Drive.
